Feel My Rhythm User Manual

Home
1

Start the Personality Quiz

Begin by launching the MBTI Personality Quiz application. You'll see the welcome screen with various people representing different personality types.

Click the "Start Quiz" button to begin answering questions about your preferences and personality traits.

2

Answer the Questions

Respond to all the questions presented in the quiz. Your answers will help determine your MBTI personality type.

Once you've completed all questions, your personality type will be revealed.

3

Review Results & Choose Next Steps

After completing the quiz, you'll be presented with two options:

  • Take the quiz again - If you want to retry or aren't satisfied with your results
  • Proceed to the playlist - Move forward to get personalized music recommendations
4

Log in to Spotify

If you choose to proceed to the playlist, you'll need to log in with your Spotify credentials.

Note: A Spotify account is required to access the personalized music recommendations.

5

Upload Photo for Mood Detection

After logging in, you can upload a photo to detect your current mood.

The application will analyze your photo and, combined with your MBTI personality type, recommend music that matches both your personality and current mood.

Important: The music recommendations are uniquely tailored based on both your MBTI personality type and your current mood as detected from your uploaded photo.
